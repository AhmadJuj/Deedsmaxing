**Project: DeedsMaxing — Ramadan Habit Tracker App**

**Stack:** React Native (Expo)

---

**SCREENS & FEATURES:**

**1. Onboarding Screen**
- Ask for username on first launch
- Ask for city (for sehri/iftar times)
- Set daily Niyyah (intention) each morning with a text input prompt

**2. Home Screen**
- Today's Hijri date display
- Daily Niyyah reminder at top
- Deed categories as tabs: Salah, Quran, Charity, Fasting, Dhikr, Family
- Each category has its own checklist:
  - Salah: Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, Taraweeh, Tahajjud
  - Quran: Pages read today, Juz tracker (cumulative)
  - Charity: Gave Sadaqah, Helped someone, Shared food
  - Fasting: Kept fast, Made sehri, Made dua at iftar
  - Dhikr: Morning Adhkar, Evening Adhkar, 100x Subhanallah, Istighfar
  - Family: Called a relative, Made dua for parents, Avoided argument
- Points per task (10 pts regular, 25 pts bonus tasks)
- Overall daily progress bar (% of all tasks done)

**3. Last 10 Nights Tracker**
- Activates on 20th Ramadan automatically
- Special nightly checklist for Laylatul Qadr preparation
- Bonus 100 pts per night in last 10
- Visual countdown to each of the odd nights (21, 23, 25, 27, 29)
- "Laylatul Qadr Achieved" badge if all odd nights completed

**4. Quran Progress Tracker**
- Input how many juz completed today
- Cumulative Ramadan juz tracker (goal: 30 juz by Eid)
- Visual progress bar toward full Quran completion
- Milestone badges: "First Juz", "Halfway", "Khatam"

**5. Streaks & Points**
- Daily streak counter (consecutive days with 50%+ tasks done)
- Total Ramadan points accumulated
- Streak freeze mechanic (earn 1 freeze every 7 days)
- Bonus multiplier on last 10 nights (2x points)

**6. Leaderboard Screen**
- Global leaderboard (username + points + streak)
- Friends leaderboard (invite via code)
- Anonymous mode toggle for humility
- Rank badges: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Diamond based on points

**7. Badges & Achievements**
- "First Deed" — complete first task

- "Generous Soul" — Sadaqah 7 days in a row
- "Quran Keeper" — Read Quran 10 days in a row
- "Laylatul Qadr Seeker" — Active all last 10 nights
- "Khatam" — Completed 30 juz
- "Iron Streak" — 20 day streak
- "Ramadan Champion" — Top 10 on leaderboard

**8. Dua & Reminder Screen**
- Sehri countdown timer + sehri dua
- Iftar countdown timer + iftar dua
- Push notifications for sehri (15 mins before) and iftar
- Daily hadith or ayah displayed

**9. Reflection Journal**
- One short note per day (max 200 chars)
- Viewable as a Ramadan diary at end
- Optional mood tracker (peaceful, struggling, motivated, grateful)

**10. Profile Screen**
- Username, avatar (emoji-based)
- Total points, current streak, best streak
- All earned badges displayed
- Full Ramadan stats summary
